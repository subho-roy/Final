About Flame On!

The endeavour of Flame On started with the mission of providing an inspired multi cuisine restaurant to the viander citizens of Pune. We wanted to give all the palaticians an unmatched culinary experience and an unrivalled hospitality. With Teritree we have planted a top fine restaurant in this city that keeps growing while staying grounded to it's roots of exquisiteness and service.

Food for us comes from relations, whether they have wings or fins or roots. That is how we consider food. Food has a culture. It has a history. It has a story. It has relationships.
